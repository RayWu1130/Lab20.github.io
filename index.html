Projects

This is a little game about guessing words which need two people. In this game, one person inputs a secret word and another guesses. The game will hint you the length of the word, and will let you know if you have the right letter in the right index (e.g., secret_word: Ray; input: Way; output: _ay). In addition, this game will calculate your score. You have 100 points at first; each guess will lose 10 points and each letter you guess correctly will gain 1 point. The game will end when you guess the word or you run out of points.
